Dry Angler Outdoor Apparel

Custom eCommerce Site (Front-End Development)

This Oregon-based outdoor apparel company needed a new site for both their retail and wholesale brands, Dry Angler Outdoors and Drift Creek Outdoors. The client was looking for a completely new look that reflected the versatility of their all-weather gear, an excellent shopping experience for online retail customers, and an easy way to manage inventory.

This bold, colorful site was built in WordPress using Woocommerce for eCommerce functionality. A whole lotta organized Sass/CSS and Woocommerce template customization using PHP brought the site to life. Customized product category pages make it easy for customers to find what they're looking for and Pods Framework allows the client to quickly select which products they would like featured on various pages.
