Hegg Memorial Health Center

Custom Site

Hegg Memorial Health Center of Rock Valley, IA needed a re-organization of their large site as well as a fresh new look.

Their new site features a mega menu so users can easily browse the list of the client's health and patient services, an events calendar and a blog. The client really took the site to the next level and added new content including a countdown timer, careers page and photo gallery.
