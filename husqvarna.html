Husqvarna Carolinas

Custom Landing Page (Front-End Development)

Husqvarna, a leading manufacturer of outdoor power products, needed a landing page to interactively display their locations in North and South Carolina as part of their online ad campaign.

The map, built using Google Maps API, interacts with the list of locations using jQuery and Backbone.js. By hovering over a map marker, the respective location info in the list is scrolled to and highlighted, and vice versa. Working closely with our back-end development team, this project was a fun and interesting challenge.
