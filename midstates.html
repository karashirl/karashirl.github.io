Mid-States Construction

Custom Site (Front-End Development)

Mid-States Construction, an Indiana commercial and residential builder, needed a refresh to their outdated site and were looking for a clean, modern design with a way to display their past projects.

Using a heavily-customized WordPress theme, the site features strong calls to action and a portfolio page to prominently display each project with individual pages for longer project descriptions, which the client can easily add/edit in the CMS.
